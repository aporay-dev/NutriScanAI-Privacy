Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 24, 2025

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

Myriad Tech Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the NutriScan AI mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using NutriScan AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Photos: Images of nutrition labels you capture or select from your photo library for analysis
  • Purchase Information: Subscription and transaction data processed through Apple's App Store

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Number of scans performed, features used, and app interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Advertising Data: Ad interactions, impressions, and advertising identifiers (if you grant tracking permission)

1.3 Camera and Photo Library Access

We request access to your camera and photo library solely to:

  • Capture photos of nutrition labels
  • Select existing photos from your library for analysis
  • Process images for nutrition information extraction

We do not store, share, or use these photos for any other purpose. Images are temporarily processed and then discarded.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Nutrition Analysis: Process nutrition label images using AI to extract nutritional information
  • Service Delivery: Provide and maintain app functionality, including scan history
  • Subscription Management: Process and manage your in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads to free users (with your consent)
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 OpenAI API

  • Purpose: AI-powered nutrition label analysis
  • Data Shared: Nutrition label images (temporarily)
  • Access Method: Via secure proxy server
  • Privacy Policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Data Retention: Images are processed in real-time and not permanently stored by OpenAI

3.2 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements to free users
  • Data Collected: Advertising identifiers, device information, ad interactions
  • Ad Personalization: Non-personalized ads by default; personalized only with your explicit consent via App Tracking Transparency (ATT)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized ads through iOS settings or by upgrading to premium

3.3 Apple App Store

  • Purpose: In-app purchase processing and subscription management
  • Data Collected: Purchase history, subscription status
  • Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In compliance with iOS 14.5+ requirements, we request your permission before tracking your activity across other apps and websites.

What happens if you allow tracking:

  • You may see more relevant ads based on your interests
  • We can measure ad effectiveness across apps
  • Google AdMob may show personalized advertisements

What happens if you deny tracking:

  • You'll see non-personalized ads only
  • Your experience with the app remains fully functional
  • All core features work exactly the same

You can change your tracking preference anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Scan history (nutrition analysis results)
  • Subscription tier and status
  • App preferences and settings
  • Scan count (for free users)

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all network communications
  • Secure proxy server for API requests
  • No permanent storage of nutrition label images
  • Local encryption for sensitive data on your device
  • Regular security audits and updates

5.3 Data Retention

  • Images: Processed immediately and permanently deleted (not stored)
  • Scan History: Stored locally on your device until you delete the app or clear data
  • Usage Analytics: Retained for 90 days for app improvement purposes
  • Purchase Records: Retained by Apple per their policies

6.2 Correction and Deletion
